About Us

Founded in 2003, NextStep Security is one of the leading IT security companies in Israel. NextStep specializes in integration of software and hardware security solutions, the design and implementation of disaster recovery and data replication solutions and risk management and security training and consulting. Our service portfolio includes: The implementation of worldwide IT security arrays, design and implementation of network access control systems and IT security solutions such as: Malicious code defense, Encryption Systems, secure remote access, intrusion detection and prevention, data replication systems. The company also conducts security surveys, penetration testing, supply training and consulting in the fields of information security and risk management.
